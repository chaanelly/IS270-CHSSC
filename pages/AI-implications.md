#AI Implications

| Issue  | Description  |
| ---  | ---  |
| Risk to archival jobs  | Some archivists are concerned that AI tools for automated metadata creation could change the profession.  |
| Cultural Sensitivity  | Both interviewees noted that describing culturally specific collections often requires contextual knowledge that AI systems cannot easily replicate. AI use can conflict with “cultural protocols or community norms” (Lo, 2026)  |
| Non-Consensual Use  | During the conversation with David, he shared that the organization has not had explicit conversations about AI, and that they need to create AI guidelines. He shared concerns about putting digital archives online because there's the risk that people will put those records through AI. Generative AI needs training data to absorb information and once absorbed its virtually impossible to identify the original source item and define provenance. Gen AI should not be allowed in the archive because, “information without origin is a liability” (Lo, 2026)  |
